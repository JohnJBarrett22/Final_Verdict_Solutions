Frequently Asked Questions

Answering your judgment enforcement questions

What type of judgments do you buy?
We buy any type of civil court judgements. This includes: Magisterial District Court, Court of Common Pleas, Superior Court, Philadelphia Municipal Court and Federal Court judgments.
How much do you pay for judgments?
Payment is based on many factors such as how old the judgment is, whether it is a "default judgment", and whether the debtor has any assets we can recover.
What size judgments will you purchase?
We will consider purchasing any judgement over $5,000.
Is there any cost to me?
No, there is no cost to you. We will thoroughly research your court judgment and provide you an offer with absolutely no obligation. If you like what you hear, your can sell your judgement to us for cash.
