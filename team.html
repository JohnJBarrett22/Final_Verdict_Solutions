Our Team

The people That Make It Happen

Our world-class team

Our team consists of a vast network of private investigators, attorneys and collection professionals across all states that help with process serving, obtaining documents from courthouses and sheriff's departments and providing detailed insight to the debtor's information and assets. This team is constantly in the know of state and local changes to execute the law to the fullest. Behind the scenes at First Verdict Solutions however, is a one-person operation led by Mr. Jamie Shelton.

Jamie Shelton

A resident and native of Pennsylvania, Jamie Shelton is the Director of Final Verdict Solutions. Over the years, he has helped hundreds of clients collect on their unpaid judgments utilizing all methods at his disposal. Cumulatively, millions have been paid to those who were awaiting their judgment and decided to do something about it.

A graduate of Case Western Reserve University, Jamie mastered business acumen. Upon graduation, he completed an apprenticeship with Evanns Collection Law, a premier collection law firm in California. In this time, he acquired extensive experience collecting judgments in all 50 states, particularly Pennsylvania, California, New Jersey, Florida, Ohio and West Virginia.

Jamie is a founder and a leading board member of the California Association of Judgment Professionals (CAJP). This organization came into existence, as Jamie and other collection professionals saw a shift in laws on the horizon that would hinder judgement collection efforts. Due to this group's efforts, judgment collection is still feasible in California.

He is an active member of The Judgment Institute of California. Despite being one of the youngest members in this professional organization, he is highly respected for his knowledge and expertise in the collections industry. He has also been a featured speaker at various legal seminars on the subject of judgment collection.

Jamie is known for his wit and sharp sense of humor, which often belies his no-nonsense attitude when it comes to getting the job done. His clients appreciate his dedication to customer service, and his willingness to go above and beyond what is expected in order to achieve success.
